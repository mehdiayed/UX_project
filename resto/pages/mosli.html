CHICKEN MOSLI

* * *

5 - chicken pieces 2 - fresh tomatoes 3 - Potatoes 3 - onions 2 - green peppers 1 C. mustard 1 C. turmeric - - salt and pepper 1 C. crushed garlic 1 glass of olive oil - - thyme and rosemary - - Hot water Preparation 1 - Peel the potatoes and cut them into pieces. 2 - Do the same with the tomatoes, peppers and onions. 3 - In a bowl, pour the oil and add the mustard, turmeric, salt and pepper and mix. 4 - In a large baking dish, put the onion, add the garlic and a little oil. 5 - Place the chicken pieces and the potatoes. 6 - Pour over the spice sauce. 7 - Add the tomato pieces and peppers. Then a little thyme and rosemary. 8 - Cover with hot water. 9 - Bake at 180° for about 40 minutes. 10 - Just before serving, garnish with a little parsley and finely chopped onion.
