LILA LBAYA is your family-run Tunisian restaurant. But we don't limit ourselves to that: we also have a bakery, café and art shop presenting the most authentic flavors and smells of our native Tunisia! At LILA LBAYA, we only use fresh and organic products directly from our local suppliers as well as from Tunisia in order to bring a unique taste and experience to all our customers with the necessary requirement appreciating homemade and good ingredients. for health. Our chef, Safwen Bensaad, brings to each of our preparations her unique style and his detailed approach in order to highlight Tunisian cuisine as you have probably never been able to experience it before. In August, discover our new opening hours. Thank you for booking to ensure a place in our little Sidi Bou cocoon! "LILA LBAYA is a culinary treasure in the middle of the hustle and bustle of London. Authentic Tunisian cuisine and great staff." - Mehdi Ayed
